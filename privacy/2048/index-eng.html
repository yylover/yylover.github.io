2048 Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 14, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the 2048 Game app collects, uses, and shares your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Device Information

  • Device Identifier (IDFA): Used for delivering personalized ads
  • Device model and operating system version: Used for optimizing app performance
  • Device language settings: Used for providing localized content

1.2 Usage Information

  • Game scores and game progress: Used for saving game state
  • Game usage frequency and duration: Used for analyzing app usage
  • Ad click data: Used for ad performance analysis

1.3 Location Information

  • We do not collect your precise location information

2. How We Use Information

2.1 App Functionality

  • Save game progress to ensure you can continue your game
  • Record game scores to provide game leaderboards
  • Optimize game performance and user experience

2.2 Ad Delivery

  • Use device identifiers (IDFA) to deliver personalized ads
  • Analyze ad performance to improve ad delivery strategies

2.3 App Improvement

  • Analyze user behavior to improve game features
  • Fix app bugs and issues
  • Develop new features and game modes

3. Information Sharing

3.1 Third-Party Services

  • Ad Service Providers: We may share device identifiers and usage information with ad service providers to deliver personalized ads
  • Analytics Service Providers: We may share anonymous usage data with analytics service providers to analyze app usage

3.2 Legal Requirements

  • We may disclose your information if required by law
  • We may disclose your information to protect our rights, property, or safety

4. Data Storage

4.1 Storage Method

  • Game data is stored locally on your device
  • No personal data is stored in the cloud

4.2 Storage Duration

  • Game data will be stored until you uninstall the app or clear app data
  • Ad-related data will be stored according to ad service providers' policies

5. User Rights

5.1 Opt Out of Personalized Ads

  • You can limit ad tracking in your device settings
  • Path: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

5.2 Delete Data

  • You can delete all game data by uninstalling the app
  • You can delete game progress and scores by clearing app data

6. Children's Privacy

  • Our app is suitable for users of all ages
  • We do not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13
  • If we discover that we have collected information from children under 13, we will delete it immediately

7. Privacy Policy Updates

  • We may update this privacy policy from time to time
  • Updated privacy policies will be posted in the app or on our website
  • Continued use of the app constitutes your agreement to the updated privacy policy

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions or suggestions about this privacy policy, please contact us through:

  • Email: privacy@game2048.com
  • Address: Haidian District, Beijing, China

9. In-App Purchases

  • We offer in-app purchase options to remove ads
  • All purchase transactions are processed through Apple App Store
  • Purchase-related information will be handled according to Apple's privacy policy
